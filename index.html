Key Takeaways

Here are the main topics that will be covered in the Student of the year.

CGPA

CGPA must be minimum of 6.0 with no active backlogs.

Attendence

Attendance must be an average of 75%

CCA

The student must participate at least in 3 or more co-curricular activities.

Gathering Information

Information is gathered by students and verified by departments and clubs.

Building Leaderboard

Poinst are given accordingly to each and every eligible student.

Evaluating Results

Top 10 listed on leaderboard and winner is finalised.
