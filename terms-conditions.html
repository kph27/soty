Only for CMRTC students

The Student of the year is selected based on their academic CGPA, attendance and active participation in co-curricular activities.This helps in overall improvement of a student performance, which encourages and motivates a student to actively participate in co-curricular activities apart from academics.

  • CGPA must be minimum of 6.0 with no active backlogs.
  • Attendance must be an average of 75%
  • The student must participate at least in 3 or more Co-Curricular Activities.
